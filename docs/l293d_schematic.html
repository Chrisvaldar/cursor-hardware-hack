L293D chip pinout (DIP-16, both U1 and U2)

        ┌─────────────────┐
  EN1 1 │●               │ 16  VCC2  ← motor battery + (6–12 V)
  IN1 2 │                 │ 15  IN4   ← ESP32 direction
 OUT1 3 │     L293D       │ 14  OUT4  → motor wire
  GND 4 │                 │ 13  GND   → common ground
  GND 5 │                 │ 12  GND   → common ground
 OUT2 6 │                 │ 11  OUT3  → motor wire
  IN2 7 │                 │ 10  IN3   ← ESP32 direction
  VCC 8 │                 │  9  EN2   ← ESP32 PWM
        └─────────────────┘
  Pin 8 VCC  = 5 V logic (from ESP32 5V pin)
  Pin 16 VCC2 = VMOT (separate battery, NOT from ESP32 3.3V)

Power wiring

5 V (red) — L293D pin 8 on U1 and U2 ← ESP32 5V pin (USB powered)
VMOT 6–12 V (orange) — L293D pin 16 on U1 and U2 ← motor battery + (e.g. 4×AA or 2S LiPo through proper BEC)
GND (black) — L293D pins 4, 5, 12, 13 + ESP32 GND + battery − all tied together
  • 100 µF capacitor between VCC2 (pin 16) and GND on each L293D, close to the chip
  • Do not power motors from ESP32 3.3 V pin
  • GPIO12 = power LED (on while firmware runs; LED + resistor to GND)

L293D truth table (what the firmware does)

IN1IN2EN (PWM)Motor
LOWLOWoffStop / coast
HIGHLOWPWMForward (speed = duty)
LOWHIGHPWMReverse

Wheel spins backward? Swap the two motor wires on OUT, or swap IN1/IN2 in config.h. Use dashboard_remote.html Motor ID to assign HW0–3 to corners.
